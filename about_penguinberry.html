What is a Penguinberry?

You may think that a Penguinberry is a penguin-shaped berry, but alas it is not (although it could be). My favorite video game series of all time is Final Fantasy and one of the most infamous monsters you can face in it is called a "Tonberry". Over the past couple of years, I have been wanting to build my own social media handle and start streaming my favorite games. I decided that my username would be a combination of one of my favorite animals and video game enemies, hence "Penguinberry" was born.

My goal is to graduate from college with a degree in multimedia and use what I have learned to help with my streaming platform business as well as provide similar services to others. In 2024, I plan on getting my stream and merchandise store set up!
